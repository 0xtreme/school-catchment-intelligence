How this works

Scoring model

Value score combines school quality (ICSEA), affordability context (SA2 housing burden), and teaching-capacity proxy (inverse student-teacher ratio).

How to use
  1. Select a state to focus school points and catchments.
  2. Filter by school sector/type and search by school name.
  3. Switch map metric to view quality, affordability, or value.
  4. Use charts to verify trade-offs before shortlisting suburbs.
Official sources used
  • ACARA My School Data Access: School Profile 2025 and School Location 2025
  • ABS Data API: Census 2021 G02 (SA2 medians) and Annual ERP ASGS 2021
  • NSW Department of Education catchments (Data NSW)
  • Victorian Government School Zones 2026 (DataVic)
  • Queensland school catchments 2025 (data.qld.gov.au)
